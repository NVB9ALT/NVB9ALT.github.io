GEOFS ADDON MANAGER


INSTRUCTIONS:


1. Select the addons you want to use. Clicking on a listing will show a window with details on the addon.


2. Press "Enter". If a popup tells you that certain addons have dependencies, add to your selection the addons indicated as dependencies and try again.


3. You will be provided with a Tampermonkey userscript and a bookmarklet that contain your selected addons. YouTube tutorials for both are linked.
