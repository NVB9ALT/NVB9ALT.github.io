GEOFS ADDON MANAGER


INSTRUCTIONS:


  • Select the addons you want to use. Clicking on a listing will show a window with details on the addon.
  • Press "Enter". If a popup tells you that certain addons have dependencies, add to your selection the addons indicated as dependencies and try again. If a popup tells you that certain addons conflict with each other, remove one of the conflicting addons and try again.
  • The site will use your selections to produce a Tampermonkey userscript and a bookmarklet that contain your selected addons. YouTube tutorials for both are linked.
